Relecov platform announcement

Relecov Platform website is ready for beta testing.

Before making it public, we have opened it to the members of the RELECOV network. If you have received access to the platform, thanks for trying everything! We are willing to receive your feedback .

This beta version is focused on showing you the platform design and how the data is stored and displayed. Our proposal is that everything inside the intranet will have limited access, and all the information in the dashboards will be public. We have set some visualization and data exploitment examples in the dashboard. We are willing to improve and grow all the data analysis with your feedback and comments so the platform would became useful for an effective surveillance based on genomics.
